OUR GOAL
Is to make a niche hobby like the observation of microbiology thru water droplets interesting using a virtual experience full of content that can intrigue other people.THE EXPERIENCE
A 3D virtual experience where the player can navigate thru many rooms that showcase non-traditional ways to display art regarding microbiology.
